Information Collection Notice

  • 1. Information Collected
    • We collect personal and technical information when you register, access the Platform, and use our features. The types of data we collect include:
    • Personal Information: Includes registration details such as name, email address, username, and password.
    • Content: When you upload and publish images on the Platform, we store those images, including any associated metadata.
    • Usage Data: We record information on how frequently Platform features are used, including the filters applied, processing times, and editing configurations.
  • 2. Purposes of Data Collection
    • The collected information is used to:
    • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform.
    • Enable users to share and publish images.
    • Enhance the user experience with personalization and recommendations.
    • Protect user and system security and integrity.
    • Comply with legal regulations and audits as necessary.
  • 3. Data Transfer
    • Your data will not be shared outside of PixelArt, except with service providers or when required by law.
  • 4. User Rights
    • You have the right to:
    • Access and correct your personal information.
    • Request deletion of your information.
    • Limit the use of your information for specific purposes.
  • 5. Procedure for Exercising Rights
    • To exercise these rights, please contact us at pixelart@support.com.
