    • We retain your personal information and content as long as you have an active account on the Platform. If you request to delete your account, we will remove your data and associated content, except when law or operational needs require retention.

    • You can access and update your information in your user profile. If you would like to delete your account or have questions about your personal information, contact us at pixelart@support.com.
